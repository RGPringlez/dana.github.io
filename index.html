Welcome, my angel

By James Pringle

Snappy

I hope you know that I genuinely do love you my angel; you are the most important person to me in this entire world and I do not know what I would do without you. You have helped me through so much and I am eternally grateful. I could go on and on about all the things I love about you:

  • your eyes
  • the way your mouth turns down eeeever so slightly when you smile
  • those deep and gorgeous brown of your eyes
  • the way you twirl your hair when you're deep in thought
  • and, last but not least, the absolute beauty and adorability with which you act

This video is one of my favourites that you have showed to me; I love it for the way in which it portrays your love of all things cute and innocent. You are my snappy my love, my kleine krokodil <3.
